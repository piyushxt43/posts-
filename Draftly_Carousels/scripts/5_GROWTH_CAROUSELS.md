## Carousel 04 — No WebGL. Just Scroll.

**Growth goal:** Developer + designer credibility · technical differentiation  
**PDF lead magnet:** Scroll engine deep dive + FPS tuning + export structure  
**Comment keyword:** `SCROLL`  
**Folder slug:** `04_No_WebGL_Just_Scroll`

### Cover
- **Headline:** no webgl · no three.js · just scroll
- **Sub:** how 400 frames become a 3d site
- **Badge:** Zero Code

### Slide 02 — The insight
- **Headline:** your video becomes a scroll experience
- **Body:** AI generates cinematic video. Hundreds of frames extract. Visitor scroll = film scrubs forward. Native browser. Every device.

### Slide 03 — Why not WebGL
- **Table:** WebGL = GPU tax, mobile jank, dev hiring · Scroll frames = 60fps feel, works everywhere
- **Body:** No runtime 3D library. Just img sequence + scroll listener.

### Slide 04 — Frame extraction
- **Labels:** Extracting Frames · 400 / 400
- **Settings:** 10–40 adjustable FPS · 36–2000 frame range
- **Tech:** FFmpeg WASM client-side

### Slide 05 — Multi-video continuation
- **Headline:** chain clips for longer scroll
- **Body:** Premium brands need 20s+ motion. End frame of clip A = start frame of clip B. Seamless scroll journey.

### Slide 06 — First / last frame control
- **Headline:** control the motion arc
- **Body:** Set opening keyframe (product closed) and closing keyframe (product hero reveal). Video interpolates between.
