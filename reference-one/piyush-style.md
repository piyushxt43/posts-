# Piyush Carousel Style (@piyush.glitch)

## Brand

- Handle: `@piyush.glitch`
- Footer on every slide: `@piyush.glitch` bottom-left, small grey sans
- Wordmark top: `PIYUSH.GLITCH` caps

## Visual modes

### Cream grid (educational slides)

- Background: `#F4EEDE` cream grid
- Headlines: bold black sans, one phrase coral `#EF5E45` or italic serif
- Cards: cream with thin border, monospace for prompts
- PDFs: same palette via `pdf_builder_rich.py`

### Sky hero (covers / CTAs)

- Bright blue sky, soft clouds, low-angle cinematic
- Model: late 20s, terracotta-rust linen outfit OR founder with MacBook
- White headline stack + white pill CTA
- Used in: `6th June/`, `Draftly_Growth/` (varied covers)

### Terracotta editorial (5th June batch)

- Background: `#C45E3B` terracotta, paper grain
- Black pill tags, white GitHub-style repo cards with tape corners
- Header: `JUN @2026  PIYUSH.GLITCH  [slide N / 08]`
- Chase H AI plugin-deck influence

### Gemini white promo (7th June)

- White background, pastel corner blobs
- Google Gemini multicolor star logo
- Comment **FREE** CTA in red

## Slide specs

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Ratio | 3:4 (1080×1440) or 4:5 (1080×1350) |
| Footer | `@piyush.glitch` or `draftly.space` per brand |
| No | slide numbers baked in, SWIPE arrows, watermarks |

## Lead magnets

| Keyword | PDF |
|---------|-----|
| Comment keyword in caption | Matching `build_pdfs.py` output |
| Examples: `BUILD`, `FREE`, `PLUGIN` | Dense operator guides, not slide recaps |

## Caption pattern

```
Hook line 1-2 sentences.

Swipe for [value].

Comment [KEYWORD] for the PDF.

@piyush.glitch

#hashtags
```
